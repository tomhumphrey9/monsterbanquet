Every year on the planet Kelthúk a gargantuan banquet is held for 7777 gluttonous monsters.
It was started a millennia ago - Holy Ruler Archon and his cabal of Synth beings arrived on the planet Kelthúk in search of an intergalactic beacon.

They were met by the native tribes of the plains - the Peons, the elder FanIr Windhelm presented their large swath of farmland and bounties of alien delicacies.

They later discovered Mount Erador, home to the Stoneskins. Chieftain Gorem Cliffchest agreed to offer his workers to build a sky palace atop the clouds of Kelthúk. All three sides came together to put on the galaxy’s most grandiose banquet event.

Beneath their feet rests an army of brutal undead monsters, commanded by a necromancer - Dathrog, the Cruel. These monsters were banished to the Grotto by Holy Ruler Archon. Dathrog and his minions will never relent until the banquet is destroyed.

Atop the clouds of Kelthúk, the Divine Citadel. This is the headquarters where they continue their search for a mysterious and powerful relic.

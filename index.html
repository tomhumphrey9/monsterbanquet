What is monster Banquet?

Monster Banquet is an NFT collection of 7777 hungry Monsters, ready to feast on the Ethereum Blockchain. 
There are four different species of Monsters, some rarer than others. 
Each Monster is generated with a smart contract, using over 300 unique hand drawn traits.
